History of Disc Golf

Early Beginnings 1926
No one really knows who invented the sport of disc golf but the first known occurence of it was in 1926. It was was in Vancouver, British Columbia where some kids played a game with tin lids. They named it Tin Lid Golf and would play it regularly on thier school grounds.
George Sappenfeld Thinks of Disc Golf 1965
One day a man named George Sappenfield was playing golf and thought to himself that his kids could play golf with frisbee discs. This idea influenced George to contact Wham-O Manufacturing to host a frisbee golf competition.
Ed Headrick 1976
Ed Headrick worked at Wham-O when he had heard of disc golf he resigned from Wham-O and started his own company called Disc Golf Association in 1976.
Professional Disc Golf Association
Disc golf became very popular. You can pretty much find a course in any state in America. After a while disc golfers got together and formed the Professional Disc Golf Association. The PDGA hosts tournaments and are guiding the progress of disc golf.
