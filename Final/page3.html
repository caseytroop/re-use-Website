Disc Types

Hello

There are many different brands of discs for disc golf. Every company makes their discs unique but there are always three main types of discs.

Here is a table of the different type of discs
Picture Type of Disc Description
Putter Putters are just your regular old frisbee disc. A lot of companies revamp them to look nice though. They are usually used for when you are close to the basket.
Mid-Range Driver Mid-Range discs are used as a driver or when you are far away from the basket. They have sharper edges that enable them to cut through the air better.
Distance Driver Drivers usually have sharp bevelled edges and have most of their mass concentrated on the outer rim of the disc rather than equally around the disc. These discs are usually for the more experienced disc golfer.
