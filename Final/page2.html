How to Play Disc Golf

...A Brief Tutorial on the Sport

Rules
The rules for disc golf are almost identical to regular golf. Each hole has a par that you are shooting for and each throw of the disc is a stroke. So say you threw 3 times and and par was 3. You got par!
The Main Goal
The main goal is to make your disc to the basket. You can do this by throwing your disc using many different throwing techniques like backhand, forehand, tomahawk etc. Once your disc is in the basket you tally up your score and move to the next whole
